Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the prescription analysis?

Our AI achieves 95% accuracy in reading standard veterinary prescriptions. However, we always recommend verifying with your veterinarian for critical medical decisions.

Is my pet's data secure?

Absolutely! We don't store your pet's prescriptions or personal information. All analysis happens in real-time and files are deleted after processing.

Can I use this for cats or other pets?

Currently, we specialize in canine healthcare. We're working on expanding to other pets soon!

What if the prescription is handwritten?

We can process clear handwritten prescriptions, but typed prescriptions yield the best results. For unclear handwriting, our support team can help manually.
